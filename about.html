About Me

I am a third year Computer Science and Mathematics major at Grinnell College.  I work for the Computer Science Peer Teaching office at the college, where I am an individual tutor for students in the intro classes.  I am also on the Men's Swimming and Diving team.

I am originally from Northfield, Minnesota.  I grew up on a hobby farm and spent most of my childhood in 4H.  I really enjoyed raising pigs for the county and state fairs, and I also did a variety of static projects including electric, computer, foods, and photography.

When I'm not coding, I like to play video games and swim.  My favorite activity to do when I am out and about is finding patterns in objects that appear to be random.
